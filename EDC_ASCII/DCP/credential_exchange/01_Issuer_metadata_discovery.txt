Descripción Técnica:

This is the bootstrapping phase where the Holder discovers the Issuer's capabilities, supported credential formats (e.g., jwt_vc_json), and cryptographic suites.

(1) HTTP GET: The Holder requests the standard .well-known endpoint defined by the DCP specification to retrieve the Issuer's metadata.

(2) Configuration: The Issuer responds with a JSON document describing its credential_configurations_supported. This includes the specific credential types (e.g., MembershipCredential), the formats supported (e.g., VC1_0_JWT), and the cryptographic algorithms available for signing.
